Author
Thought Leader James Victore is an American designer, born 1962. He is known for his books "Feck Perfuction" and "James Victore, Who Died and Made You the Boss". He is most notable for his unique and thought provoking posters. His design firm is small (3 Employees), and ethos of "good work" and design seem to supercede any financial motivations.

Victore gives Typographical tours through New York, tackling questions of design decisions of brands and small businesses in their natural habitat while driving the streets in a green military vehicle to survey the land.

On his site James Victore implores people to: "Have a Damn Opinion", his work often seemes clear, loud, and full of intention he is known for his bold, radical, and minimal typogrophy.
